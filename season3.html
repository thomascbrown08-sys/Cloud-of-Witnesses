Season III · 7 Studies · Grace & the Reformation

Grace Alone

How did a thousand years of accumulated religion nearly bury the gospel — and how did God recover it? This season traces the theology of grace from Augustine's restless heart through the Reformation's great rediscovery, asks the hard questions the Reformers wrestled with, and sits with the biblical conviction that salvation is entirely God's gift, received by faith, producing a life no mere rule-keeping could ever generate.
