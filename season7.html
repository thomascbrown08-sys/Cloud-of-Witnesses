Season VII · 4 Studies · Family Grief

Dark Chapters

Every family has chapters it would rather skip. The church is no different. The Crusades, the Inquisition, the prosperity gospel, the church's entanglement with slavery — these are not footnotes or exceptions. They are part of the story, and they must be named. Not to condemn the whole, but because a family that cannot grieve its worst chapters honestly is a family that cannot be trusted with its best ones. We enter these studies the way a family enters a hard conversation: with honesty, with grief, and with the conviction that God is not finished with us yet.
