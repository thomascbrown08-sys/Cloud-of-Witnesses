How Shall We Then Live?

The gospel that justifies also transforms — and the transformation was never meant to stay private. This season follows believers who let grace reshape not just their hearts but the world around them: a parliamentarian who spent his life fighting slavery, a friar who embraced poverty, a theologian who died resisting a dictator, a woman who spent forty years protecting children, a preacher who built a city's conscience, a pastor who applied the gospel to racial dignity, and a look at the civilizational gifts the Reformation left behind. Grace at work in the world.
