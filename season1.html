Who Is This Jesus?

Before the Reformation, before the missions movement, before almost anything else we will study together, the early church had to answer the most urgent question in history: Who exactly is Jesus? The battles were fierce, the stakes were eternal, and the people who fought them were more interesting than almost anyone has told you. This season begins at the very foundation.
