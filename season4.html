The Church in Every Nation

The gospel has never stayed where it started. From a Celtic monk who lit fires across pagan Ireland to two brothers who invented an alphabet so a whole people could read Scripture; from a cobbler who turned the world of missions upside down to a young woman who gave her life to a people she came to love — this season traces the extraordinary, costly, unstoppable spread of the church across every border it was never supposed to cross.
