Cloud of Witnesses

Meet the men and women whose faith, courage, and sacrifice shaped the church you belong to — and discover how their story is still yours.

"Therefore, since we are surrounded by such a great cloud of witnesses, let us run with perseverance the race marked out for us." — Hebrews 12:1

Welcome to your family history. The Christian faith did not begin with us, and it will not end with us. For two thousand years, ordinary men and women in extraordinary circumstances held on to what was true, passed it to those who came after them, and changed the world in the process. This curriculum introduces you to the figures behind the great doctrines of the church — not as marble saints, but as real people, from every corner of the globe, who faced real struggles and loved the same Jesus you do. Each study takes about 15 minutes, ends with a reflection question and a prayer, and points you somewhere to go deeper if you want more.
